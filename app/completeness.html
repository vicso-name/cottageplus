What is the cost per square meter of equipment in Canadian homes?

The company "Cottage Plus" offers several sets of houses built according to the Canadian technology. Whichever option you choose, the invariable advantage of the house of sip panels is the price per m2. Ukraine not so long ago learned about the benefits of such construction, but today in our country there are more and more energy-efficient, affordable, rapid construction of houses.

The difference is in the set of options provided. We give you the opportunity to choose the construction option according to your financial capabilities and wishes. Let's consider each of the offers.

Is it possible to change the configuration?

Any configuration of a CIP panel house offered here, the price of which depends on many aspects, is just a starting point. We never limit our customers in their wishes and are ready to meet you halfway. You can change both the variation of the house design and the finishing materials or engineering components. If you want to change the thickness of a floor or a roof, or the type and strength of the foundation, we can do this according to your wishes and financial capabilities, but on the condition that this does not contradict the regulations and does not affect the energy efficiency of the house. If the customer wants to substitute materials, we will recommend cheaper, but no less qualitative and with similar characteristics.

Whichever set of the Canadian house has been chosen (the price will be stipulated individually with each client), you can add to it additional work or use additional materials or exclude them. We also consider the option of working on the house at any stage of its construction: for example, delivery of a ready-to-install house kit or assembling the house on the previously built foundation.
